The Story

Why This Transition Makes Sense

As a Power BI Lead you sit closest to the business — but the data that arrives on your desk is often broken and you have no control over it. Analytics Engineers own the full lifecycle: raw table → clean model → dashboard. That's the role worth moving into.

💡

Your Power BI experience is a differentiator, not a handicap. Most AEs cannot build a good dashboard. The ability to own both the pipeline and the presentation layer is what separates you from engineers who only know the backend.
